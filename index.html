Baking Assistance

Multiple Shapes/Layers

Browse through a huge collection of Layer Shapes. Once you select your shape you are presented with various sizes of that shape.

Ingredients, Times & Temperatures

Selecting your Shape and Size gives you quick access to serving sizes, ingredients, cooking times, batter, icing, and more. Once you make a decision just a single-click and the layer is added to your project.

Build Quotes

Quotes are really easy in Baker's Buddy. You simply set your cost-per-serving and add any additional line items (such as fondant, filling, delivery, etc.).
That's it!

Track Payments

Payments are simple and easy to add and the remaining balance is automatically calculated. There is also a slider on the 'Home Screen' that gives you a quick visual of the total payments received and the remaining balance due.

Manage Contacts

Capture Contact Information, Set Due Dates, Flag Pickup/Delivery and more. Then from the 'Home Screen' you can easily Call, SMS, or Email with a single-click.
